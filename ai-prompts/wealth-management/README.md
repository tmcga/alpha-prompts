# Wealth Management & Private Banking -- AI Prompt Library

## Overview

Wealth management sits at the intersection of investment management, financial planning, tax optimization, and behavioral finance. Unlike institutional asset management, wealth management is fundamentally **client-centric** rather than product-centric. Every recommendation must pass through the lens of an individual's or family's unique circumstances: tax situation, estate plan, liquidity needs, risk capacity (not just tolerance), and multi-generational goals.

This section provides prompt templates tailored to the advisory workflows of private bankers, wealth advisors, financial planners, trust and estate attorneys, and family office professionals.

## Key Differences from Institutional Asset Management

| Dimension | Institutional AM | Wealth Management |
|---|---|---|
| **Tax sensitivity** | Generally tax-exempt (pensions, endowments) | Tax drag is the largest controllable cost; after-tax alpha is what matters |
| **Time horizon** | Single entity, perpetual or defined | Multi-generational; overlapping horizons per goal |
| **Behavioral finance** | Committee-based decisions | Individual/family emotions, biases, and dynamics |
| **Liquidity needs** | Predictable (actuarial, spending policy) | Lumpy and often unpredictable (home purchase, divorce, business sale) |
| **Estate planning** | N/A | Central to strategy; wealth transfer, trusts, charitable giving |
| **Goals-based framing** | Total return vs. benchmark | Safety / Lifestyle / Aspirational buckets |
| **Regulatory** | ERISA, investment company rules | Reg BI / fiduciary duty, state trust law, tax code |

## How to Use These Prompts

1. **Select the relevant file** based on the advisory workflow you are supporting.
2. **Fill in bracket placeholders** `[like this]` with client-specific data.
3. **Chain prompts** across files for holistic advice -- e.g., combine estate-tax-planning with portfolio-construction for a tax-aware rebalancing plan.
4. **Adapt the persona block** at the top of each file to match your firm's tone, compliance requirements, and target client segment.
