# Wall Street AI Prompt Suite

The most comprehensive open-source collection of AI prompt templates for finance professionals. 50+ prompt libraries covering every major desk, division, and role across Wall Street — from investment banking to trading to venture capital to wealth management.

Every prompt includes role-specific context, mathematical frameworks, and fill-in-the-blank templates designed for Claude and other LLMs.

## How to Use

1. **Start with the [Cross-Reference Guide](cross-reference-guide.md)** to see how different desks approach the same problem
2. **Pick your desk** from the navigation above
3. **Copy the role context prompt** at the top of each file — it calibrates the AI's persona
4. **Fill in the brackets** in any prompt template with your specific data
5. **Combine prompts** across desks when your analysis crosses boundaries

These prompts work with Claude (Anthropic), ChatGPT, and other LLMs. They are starting points — customize for your specific use case.
